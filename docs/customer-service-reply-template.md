# 客服回复模板

## 文档用途

本文档用于沉淀 TikTok Shop、Amazon、Shopify、Temu 等平台的客服回复模板，帮助客服在售前咨询、物流问题、退货退款、产品问题、差评安抚和售后跟进中保持回复一致、清晰、合规。

## 适用场景

- 售前商品咨询
- 尺码、规格、材质、配件咨询
- 物流进度与包裹异常咨询
- 取消订单、退款、退货、补发咨询
- 产品质量或描述不符反馈
- 差评、低星评价和客户情绪安抚
- 售后处理进度跟进
- 平台争议或投诉前的沟通

## 客服回复基本原则

- 先确认客户问题，再给出下一步动作。
- 回复语气保持礼貌、简短、清楚。
- 不承诺无法确认的结果。
- 不使用绝对化、医疗化、夸大功效或诱导评价表达。
- 涉及退款、退货、补发、赔付时，以平台政策和店铺规则为准。
- 涉及高风险投诉时，先记录问题，再升级处理。

## 售前咨询回复模板

### 商品规格咨询

Hi, thanks for reaching out. This item comes in the listed size, color, and specification shown on the product page. Please check the product details before placing your order. If you need help comparing options, we are happy to assist.

### 尺码咨询

Hi, thanks for your message. Please refer to the size chart on the product page before ordering. If you are between sizes, we recommend checking the measurements carefully and choosing based on your usual fit preference.

### 材质咨询

Hi, thanks for asking. The product material is listed in the product details. Please review the product page before purchase, and let us know if you need help confirming a specific feature.

## 物流问题回复模板

### 物流正常在途

Hi, your order is currently in transit. Tracking updates may take some time to refresh. Please keep an eye on the tracking page, and we will continue to monitor the shipment.

### 物流长时间未更新

Hi, thanks for letting us know. We will check the tracking status and follow up with the carrier if needed. Please allow us some time to verify the latest shipment information.

### 包裹显示签收但客户未收到

Hi, we are sorry to hear that. Please first check with family members, neighbors, building reception, or the delivery area. We will also review the tracking information and assist with the next steps according to the platform policy.

## 退货退款回复模板

### 客户申请退款

Hi, we are sorry the order did not meet your expectations. Please submit your request through the platform order page so it can be processed according to the platform policy. We will review it as soon as possible.

### 客户申请退货

Hi, thanks for contacting us. Please follow the return instructions on the platform order page. Once the return request is submitted, we will review it based on the platform policy and order status.

### 客户要求补发

Hi, we are sorry for the inconvenience. Please provide clear photos or details of the issue so we can verify what happened. After confirmation, we will advise the next step according to the order situation and platform policy.

## 产品问题回复模板

### 商品损坏

Hi, we are sorry to hear that the item arrived damaged. Please send clear photos of the product, packaging, and shipping label so we can review the issue and assist with the next step.

### 商品少件或错发

Hi, we apologize for the inconvenience. Please send photos of the item received, package contents, and shipping label. We will verify the order details and help resolve the issue.

### 商品描述不符

Hi, thank you for sharing this feedback. Please provide photos or a short description of the difference you noticed. We will review the product page and order information carefully.

## 差评安抚回复模板

Hi, we are sorry that your experience did not meet expectations. We appreciate your feedback and would like to understand the issue better. Please contact us through your order page so we can review the situation and assist you properly.

## 售后跟进模板

Hi, we are following up on your previous issue. We are still reviewing the information and will update you as soon as there is progress. Thank you for your patience.

## 禁止承诺和合规话术

避免使用以下表达：

- Guaranteed result
- 100% safe
- Best on the market
- Cure / treatment / medical claims
- We promise a refund outside platform policy
- Leave a positive review and we will compensate you
- This will definitely arrive by a specific date unless confirmed by platform tracking

建议使用：

- We will review the issue according to platform policy.
- We will assist with the next step after verification.
- Please provide photos or details so we can check further.
- Thank you for your patience while we review the case.
